Cub Scouts Rocket Day

Cub Scouts Rocket Day is a STEM outreach event that our competitive rocket team hosts for the younger generation to help get them interested in aerospace and STEM. We go out to local schools and help kids set up and fly their own model rocket, show off and inform our hybrid rockets, and act as a positive role model.

The Big Event

If you go to TAMU, you’ve heard about the big event quite a bit. During previous years, our competitive rocket team also forms a team to participate in the big event. We give a “thanks” back to the surrounding community by providing help and community services to those who need.
